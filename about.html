About Us

Who Are We?

R2L&L is a student-led, non-profit initiative founded with the goal of advocating for the lives and basic rights, primarily education, of women and children around the world who are unjustly deprived of them.

The Team

Sana Rauf

Co-Founder

Hi there! My name is Sana A. Rauf, and I am the co-secretary-general of R2L&L. For me, creating this initiative has been a dream of mine since I was in the ninth grade: to advocate for a cause that I’m deeply passionate about. Aside from R2L&L, I also serve as Secretary-General of my school's Model UN chapter, I am a Co-Minister of Records and Finances for our Student Advisory Council, and am an active member of the Mississauga Center Youth Council.

Alyssa D'Souza

Co-Founder

Hello! My name is Alyssa and together Sana and I founded R2L&L to advocate for people around the world who deserve the same opportunities we have, but aren't privileged to have them. We were given the chance to give back during the pandemic, and I'm proud of what we started. Outside of R2L&L and school, I also love drawing, digital art, and music.
