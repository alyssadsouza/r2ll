We are in partnership with Plan International Canada to support an initiative they operate called Gifts of Hope. You can choose to support a specific Gift of Hope and donate by clicking one of the icons below.

Where Your Money Goes

All of your money goes directly to Plan International Canada to fund one of the various Gifts of Hope our foundation supports. 80% of proceeds support programs benefiting children and families, while the other 20% goes to operating and administration costs.
